\caption{
Workflow used to map current extent and restoration suitability of wet woodland in
England. Current extent was estimated using remote-sensing embeddings and terrain
covariates in an XGBoost classifier. Restoration suitability was estimated using
LiDAR-derived terrain and wetness metrics, SMUK soil-moisture summaries, peat
depth, and distance to water in a MaxEnt/Elapid framework.
